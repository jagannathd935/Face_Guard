How FaceGuard Works

🧑‍🏫

1. Create Session

The teacher generates a secure, time-limited 6-digit session code in their dedicated dashboard and shares it with the class.

📷

2. Verify Identity

Students enter the session code and their camera activates. FaceGuard uses AI to ensure it's a live person and matches their face to internal records.

✅

3. Mark Present

Once identity and liveness are confirmed, attendance is instantly and immutably recorded in the database, with analytics available for the teacher.
